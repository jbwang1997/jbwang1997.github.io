Jiabao Wang (王家宝) is a PhD student at Nankai University, advised by Prof. Ming-Ming Cheng and Prof. Qibin Hou. His research interests include computer vision and deep learning, particularly focusing on 2D/3D/Oriented object detection, occupancy prediction and forecasting in autonomuos driving, etc. He is also currently learning the knowledge about image synthesis and generative models.
